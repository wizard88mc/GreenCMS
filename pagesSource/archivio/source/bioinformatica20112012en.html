Contents

1) Introduction to Biochemistry and Molecular Biology. Usage of molecular data banks.
2) Sequence alignment of nucleic acids and proteins, substitution matrices, exact and heuristic alignment methods, fasta, blast, multiple alignment.
3) Molecular evolution and phylogenesis. UPGMA, Neighbor Joining and Maximum Parsimony algorithms.
4) Structural analysis of proteins, protein structure databases, visualization programs, structural similarity searches.
5) Prediction of structural features from sequence, machine learning. 3D structure prediction, homology modelling and fold recognition.
6) Pattern and functional motif searches. Structural determinants of function and functional classification. Systems Biology.
