Contents

- Floating-point system and error propagation (concepts of conditioning and stability by examples).
- Complexity and efficiency of numerical algorithms (by examples).
- Numerical solution of nonlinear equations: bisection method, iterative linearization (tangents, chords, secants), fixed-point iterations.
- Numerical linear algebra: matrix conditioning, LU and QR factorizations, application to the solution of linear systems.
- Interpolation and approximation of functions and data: polynomial and piecewise polynomial interpolation, spline interpolation, least squares.
- Numerical differentiation and integration: differentiation by difference formulas, splines and least squares, interpolatory and composite quadrature formulas, asymptotic expansions and extrapolation.
- Laboratory: introduction to numerical computing with Matlab/Octave.

Suggested Courses: Algebra e Geometria, Programmazione

Required Courses: Analisi matematica
