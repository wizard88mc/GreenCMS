Contents

In contrast with mod. A, which is predominantly made of theoretical and foundational lectures, mod. B explores some specific techniques in the form of class exercises, typically accompanied by explanatory lectures. A considerable proportion of the class hours is devoted to the execution of formal reviews of project progress, scheduled at predefined milestones agreed with the groups at the end of mod. A.
