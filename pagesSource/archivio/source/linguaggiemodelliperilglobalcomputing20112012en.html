Contents

The course will focus on the following themes:

- Introduction to concurrency: from automata to reactive and
concurrent systems

- The \"Calculus of Communicating Systems\" (CCS). Transition systems
and bisimulation. Hennessy-Milner logic and tools for
verification. Mutual exclusion, deadlock, fairness. Safety and
liveness properties. Verification with the Concurrency Workbench.

- Systems with evolving topology and mobility: pi-calculus. Access
control and types. The Mobility Workbench. Relation with advanced
languages for concurrency (es. Google Go, Erlang).
