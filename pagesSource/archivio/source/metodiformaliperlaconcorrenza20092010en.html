Contents

The course will focus on the following topics: Introduction to concurrency: from automata to reactive and concurrent systems; The"calculus of communicating systems" (CCS); Transition systems and bisimulation; Hennessy-Milner logic and tools for verification; From CCS to calculi for mobility (pi-calculus, ambient calculus, etc.); Petri nets: concurrent semantics and analysis techniques, event structures and prime algebraic domains; Graph rewriting systems as models for concurrency.
