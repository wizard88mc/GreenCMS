Contents

The programming language used is (the imperative part of) C++. The notions taught are as follows: Basic types, variables, declarations and scope of the variables. Basic instructions, pointers and references. Array. Functions and parameter passing methods. Enumeration and structures. Exceptions. Cast. Recursion. Dynamic memory. Linked lists and binary trees. Recursion on lists and trees. Overloading. Separate compilation and namespaces.
