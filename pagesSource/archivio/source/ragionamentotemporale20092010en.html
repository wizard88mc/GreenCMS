Contents

Definition of quantitative and qualitative temporal constraints, temporal constraint problems, solutions, satisfiability problem (ISAT) and minimum labelling problem (MLP).
Qualitative approach: interval algebra, point algebra, macro relations.
Quantitative approach: distance algebra, metric point constraints, solving ISAT and MLP with path consistency, disjunctive temporal constraint problems.
Uncertainty: temporal constraints with uncertainty and conditional temporal problems.
Preferences: temporal constraint with fuzzy and utilitarian preferences.
Practical applications.
