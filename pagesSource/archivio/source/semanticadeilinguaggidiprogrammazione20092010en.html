Contents

The main topics of the course are as follows: Syntax and semantics of programs. Small step and big step operational semantics. Induction principles and inductive definitions. Denotational semantics. Orders, domains and fixed points. Axiomatic semantics. Program correctness by axiomatic semantics. Operational semantics of functional languages. Type systems. Type inference.
