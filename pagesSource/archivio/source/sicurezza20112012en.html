Contents

ASN.1 Synax, Standard PKCS focusing on PKCS#1, PKCS#5, PKCS#8, PKCS#7, PKCS#10 and PKCS12; Digital Signatures adopted by DigitPA (CAdES, XAdES, PadES). Use of standard de-facto libraries, Bouncy Castle and openssl, and their integration in in JAVA, C++, and .NET.
The added value of X.509 certificates in routing protocols, in network access protocols (EAP + RADIUS) and in VPN Protocols.
